### Mission

Tokenburner builds infrastructure for people who want to ship real software without becoming infrastructure engineers. The best stack is the one you don't have to think about — it just works, stays cheap, and gets out of your way.

### Philosophy

**Long term.** Your infrastructure should outlast any individual tool or trend. We build on AWS primitives that have been stable for a decade and will be stable for the next one.

**Long distance.** Deploy once and walk away for months. No servers to patch, no containers to restart, no bills that creep up while you sleep.

**Low commitment.** Start for 42 cents a month. Scale up when you need to, not because your infrastructure demands it. No vendor lock-in beyond AWS itself.

**Casual.** Infrastructure should feel like a conversation, not a contract. Describe what you want to an AI assistant, deploy with one command, iterate in plain English.

### How we think about AI

AI assistants are good at writing code when they have clear context about what exists and what's expected. Tokenburner products ship with context files that keep AI aligned across sessions — your assistant knows the schema, the conventions, and the deployment target before you type a single prompt.

We don't build AI into the stack. We build stacks that AI can work with.
